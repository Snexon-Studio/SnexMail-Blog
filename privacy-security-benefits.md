## The Privacy Problem with Traditional Email Services

Most traditional email services require personal information during registration, including your name, phone number, and sometimes even government-issued identification. This information is stored on their servers and can be accessed by:
- Company employees
- Government agencies through legal requests
- Cybercriminals through data breaches
- Third-party partners and advertisers

Once your personal information is linked to an email address, it becomes part of a permanent digital profile that can be tracked, analyzed, and potentially exploited.
